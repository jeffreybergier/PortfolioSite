Genius

November 2007 – June 2009

This was the ideal job while earning my Industrial Design degree. I worked full time for Apple and went to school full time. I witnessed the slow evolution of Apple's industrial design by working inside most generations of most of Apple's products. The refinement of internal components over time proved to me that everything can and should be improved, even if zero customers will ever see it.

The other major aspect of this position was helping to teach users how to use and troubleshoot their computers. I developed a strong empathetic “core” while working with Genius Bar customers. I had always grown up with Computers and they were always easy for me to learn. As a genius, I realized that, not only is that not true for most people, but that most computer usage paradigms are arbitrary. This makes them hard to teach and hard to learn. I bring this sense of empathy with me wherever I go.
